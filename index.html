About Us

Who We Are

Matuu Mission Health Center, located in Matuu town, Machakos County, is a modern 30-bed facility managed by the Sisters of Our Lady of the Missions since 1997. Initially serving the underprivileged in Yatta Plateau, we now offer high-quality, accessible healthcare with a focus on compassionate, professional care.

By the example of Jesus, the healer, our skilled staff provides comprehensive preventive and curative services while upholding patient dignity and confidentiality. As a leading mission hospital in Machakos County, we are dedicated to offering affordable medical services to all. Learn More
