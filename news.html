Community Outreach at Mauini

Sammy Peter 23 August 2024
05 Comments 33K Views

On 23 August 2024, Matuu Mission Health Center organized a successful community outreach program in Mauini, aimed at providing essential healthcare services to underserved areas. Our medical team offered free health screenings, consultations, and health education sessions to over 500 community members, addressing their immediate health concerns and providing preventive care advice.

The outreach was an opportunity for our healthcare professionals to connect with the community, raise awareness about common health issues, and offer guidance on maintaining long-term well-being. Through collaborations with local leaders and volunteers, we were able to deliver impactful services that promote healthier living.

We are committed to extending our services beyond our facility to ensure that everyone has access to essential healthcare. The positive feedback from the community strengthens our resolve to continue supporting initiatives like this, helping to build a healthier future for all.

“It’s our mission to make healthcare accessible to every individual in our community, especially those in remote areas.”

Matuu Mission Health Center remains dedicated to improving the health and well-being of the communities we serve. We look forward to organizing more outreach events in the future, bringing healthcare closer to home for everyone.
