About Us

Matuu Mission Dispensary is a faith-based organization located in Yatta District, Eastern Province. It is managed by the Sisters of Our Lady of the Missions and commenced operations in 1997. Initially, its aim was to assist the poor and underprivileged people in Yatta Plateau. To achieve this, the dispensary started with preventive and curative treatments, along with outreach clinics.

Following the example of Jesus, the healer, the Sisters of Our Lady of the Missions are dedicated to promoting life for all by providing compassionate care and maintaining a listening heart. Our staff members are committed to upholding high professional standards, respecting confidentiality, and collaborating with other health providers. Currently, the facility offers accessible, quality, and affordable services—both preventive and curative—to all clients, regardless of their status.

Our Comprehensive Services

At Matuu Mission Health Center, we are committed to offering a wide range of healthcare services designed to meet the diverse needs of our community. Our modern 30-bed facility in Matuu town, Machakos County, has been managed by the Sisters of Our Lady of the Missions since 1997. We combine high-quality medical care with compassion and professionalism, making healthcare accessible and effective for everyone.

Our services include:

  1. Outpatient Services: Comprehensive care for patients not admitted overnight, focusing on diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up.
  2. Antenatal and Postnatal Services: Specialized care for expectant and new mothers to ensure a healthy pregnancy and postpartum period.
  3. Dental Clinic (Every Tuesday): Quality dental care services provided every Tuesday for maintaining oral health.
  4. CCC Clinic: Comprehensive care and counseling for managing chronic conditions and diseases.
  5. Eye Clinic (1st Wednesday of the Month): Specialized eye care services available on the first Wednesday of each month.
  6. Inpatient Services: Complete care for patients admitted overnight, including treatment and recovery support.
  7. Diabetes Clinic: Comprehensive management and support for individuals with diabetes, including education and personalized care.
  8. Ultrasound Services: State-of-the-art diagnostic imaging to support accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

Guided by the example of Jesus, the healer, our dedicated staff upholds patient dignity and confidentiality. We strive to deliver affordable medical care to everyone in need, making a positive impact on the health and well-being of our community.
