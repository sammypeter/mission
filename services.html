SERVICES OFFERED

At Matuu Mission Health Center, we provide a wide range of healthcare services to meet the medical needs of our community. Explore our comprehensive offerings to enhance your health and well-being.

Comprehensive Care Clinic (CCC)

Our CCC provides specialized care for patients living with chronic conditions such as HIV/AIDS. We offer counseling, regular monitoring, and treatment support to improve health outcomes and quality of life.

Eye Clinic - 1st Wednesday of the Month

Our specialized eye clinic is available on the first Wednesday of every month. We provide eye exams, treatments for common eye conditions, and referrals for more advanced care when necessary.

Inpatient Services

Our inpatient department offers 24/7 care for patients requiring extended medical attention. From acute treatments to recovery and rehabilitation, we ensure patients receive personalized and continuous care in a comfortable environment.

Dental Clinic - Every Tuesday

Our dental clinic operates every Tuesday, offering a wide range of dental care services, from routine cleanings and exams to more specialized treatments such as tooth extractions and cavity fillings.

Diabetes Clinic

Our dedicated diabetes clinic provides specialized care for patients managing diabetes. We offer regular check-ups, education on lifestyle management, and treatment plans to help manage blood sugar levels effectively.

Ultrasound Services

We offer advanced ultrasound services for diagnostic imaging, prenatal monitoring, and the evaluation of various medical conditions. Our skilled technicians ensure accurate results to support proper diagnosis and treatment.

Maternal & Child Health

We provide comprehensive maternal and child healthcare services, including antenatal, postnatal care, immunizations, and child growth monitoring to ensure healthy mothers and babies.

Outpatient Services

Our outpatient services provide comprehensive care for a range of health concerns without requiring an overnight stay. From general consultations to diagnostic tests and minor treatments, we ensure timely and personalized care to support your health needs efficiently.

Laboratory Services

Our fully equipped laboratory offers a wide range of diagnostic tests to support medical diagnoses. Our team of professionals ensures accurate and timely results for effective patient care.

About Us

Matuu Mission Health Center, established in 1997 in Matuu town, Machakos County, is a faith-based facility dedicated to providing high-quality, compassionate care.
